01 — Executive Summary

This report presents a strategic digital channel analysis of Nike benchmarked against its primary competitor Adidas, commissioned as a digital marketing consulting engagement. Drawing on the e-SERVQUAL, WebQual, MSQ, and OVP frameworks alongside real-time data from SEMrush and SimilarWeb, the analysis evaluates both brands across corporate websites, mobile application ecosystems, and social media channels.

Nike demonstrates superior technical performance, omnichannel integration, app ecosystem maturity, and personalisation infrastructure. Adidas leads in emotional storytelling, sustainability positioning, and Gen Z cultural relevance on emerging platforms. Strategic recommendations focus on AI-driven personalisation at scale and community-led UGC campaigns to consolidate Nike's digital advantage.

03 — Analysis Frameworks
01
📊
e-SERVQUAL
Zeithaml et al., 2000
Evaluates digital service quality across efficiency, reliability, fulfilment, privacy, and responsiveness — applied to both brands' e-commerce platforms and mobile apps.
02
🖥️
WebQual
Loiacono et al., 2007
Assesses website usability, information quality, and service interaction. Key for evaluating navigation, search, trust signals, and product display standards.
03
📱
MSQ Framework
Wang et al., 2019
Mobile Service Quality — measures usability, interactivity, value-added services, and security across both brands' app ecosystems on iOS and Android.
04
⚡
SWOT Analysis
Kotler et al., 2019
Strategic mapping of internal strengths and weaknesses alongside external opportunities and threats — applied per brand for comparative digital benchmarking.
05
🎯
OVP Analysis
Chaffey & Ellis-Chadwick, 2019
Online Value Proposition — assesses the unique digital value each brand delivers online through exclusivity, personalisation, community, and innovation pillars.
06
🔍
CX Principles
Lemon & Verhoef, 2016
Customer Experience — evaluates touchpoint consistency, content relevance, usability, and mobile responsiveness across the full digital customer journey.

05 — SWOT Snapshot (Nike)
S
Strengths
Internal advantages
World-class app ecosystem (Nike App, SNKRS, NTC, NRC) with 4.8★ iOS rating
AI-powered Nike Fit sizing with AR — reduces returns and boosts purchase confidence
Geo-personalised homepage and product recommendations at scale
Over 40% DTC revenue — reducing reliance on third-party retail
Global athlete partnerships (Sam Kerr, LeBron James) driving aspirational brand equity
W
Weaknesses
Internal gaps
AR/VR try-on features less developed compared to Adidas WebAR rollout
Influencer localisation weaker in niche and regional markets
App fragmentation risk as multiple platforms can dilute user experience
Sustainability messaging less prominent vs Adidas "End Plastic Waste" positioning
O
Opportunities
External potential
Scale AI-driven personalisation engine beyond homepage to email, app flows, and push
Expand AR try-on to broader product catalogue (apparel, not just footwear)
Deepen TikTok and micro-influencer strategy to capture Gen Z at scale
Strengthen sustainability narrative to compete with Adidas' green brand equity
T
Threats
External risks
Adidas rapidly closing the gap in personalisation and AI styling tools
Increasing digital advertising costs compress DTC margin efficiency
Sustainability pressure — consumers expect more from a brand of Nike's scale
App proliferation risk — multiple apps may fragment user loyalty vs unified Adidas app

07 — Strategic Recommendations for Nike
01
Deploy Cross-Platform AI Personalisation Engine
Dynamically personalise homepages, product recommendations, email flows, and push notifications using Nike's existing AI and data infrastructure. Target a 15% increase in repeat purchase rate and 10% reduction in bounce rate within 6 months.
Target Impact
+15% RPR
02
Launch Gen Z UGC Movement on TikTok & Instagram
Activate micro-influencers and community athletes through branded challenges and creator-driven campaigns. Targets: 10% growth in Gen Z followers and 25% improvement in engagement rate within 6 months, countering Adidas's strong TikTok presence.
Target Impact
+25% Engage
03
Expand AR Try-On Across Full Product Catalogue
Scale Nike Fit's AR capabilities beyond footwear to apparel and accessories, and deploy WebAR on the website to close the gap with Adidas's AR Try-On advantage. Expected to reduce return rates and increase conversion on high-consideration items.
Target Impact
−12% Returns
04
Strengthen Sustainability Digital Narrative
Introduce sustainability product filters, green badges, and purpose-led content series across website and apps. Partner with Australian environmental initiatives to build authentic ESG positioning and counter Adidas's "End Plastic Waste" brand equity.
