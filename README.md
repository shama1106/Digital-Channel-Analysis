## 📄 Executive Summary

This report presents a strategic digital channel analysis of **Nike** benchmarked against its primary competitor **Adidas**, approached as a digital marketing consulting engagement.

Drawing on **e-SERVQUAL**, **WebQual**, **MSQ**, and **OVP** frameworks alongside real-time data from SEMrush and SimilarWeb, the analysis evaluates both brands across corporate websites, mobile application ecosystems, and social media channels.

Nike demonstrates **superior technical performance**, omnichannel integration, app ecosystem maturity, and personalisation infrastructure. Adidas leads in **emotional storytelling**, sustainability positioning, and Gen Z cultural relevance on emerging platforms.

Strategic recommendations focus on AI-driven personalisation at scale and community-led UGC campaigns to consolidate Nike's digital advantage.

## ⚡ SWOT Snapshot — Nike

**Strengths**
- World-class app ecosystem (Nike App, SNKRS, NTC, NRC) with 4.8★ iOS rating
- AI-powered Nike Fit sizing with AR — reduces returns and boosts purchase confidence
- Geo-personalised homepage and product recommendations at scale
- Over 40% DTC revenue — reducing reliance on third-party retail

**Weaknesses**
- AR/VR try-on features less developed compared to Adidas WebAR rollout
- Sustainability messaging less prominent vs Adidas "End Plastic Waste" positioning
- App fragmentation risk across multiple platforms

**Opportunities**
- Scale AI-driven personalisation beyond homepage to email, app flows and push notifications
- Expand AR try-on to full product catalogue — apparel, not just footwear
- Deepen TikTok and micro-influencer strategy to capture Gen Z at scale

**Threats**
- Adidas rapidly closing the gap in personalisation and AR innovation
- Increasing digital advertising costs compressing DTC margin efficiency
- Sustainability pressure from consumers expecting more from a brand of Nike's scale

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

**01 — Deploy Cross-Platform AI Personalisation Engine**
> Dynamically personalise homepages, product recommendations, email flows, and push notifications. Target: **+15% repeat purchase rate** and **−10% bounce rate** within 6 months.

**02 — Launch Gen Z UGC Movement on TikTok & Instagram**
> Activate micro-influencers and community athletes through branded challenges. Target: **+10% Gen Z followers** and **+25% engagement rate** within 6 months.

**03 — Expand AR Try-On Across Full Product Catalogue**
> Scale Nike Fit AR beyond footwear to apparel and deploy WebAR on site. Target: **−12% return rate** on high-consideration product categories.

**04 — Strengthen Sustainability Digital Narrative**
> Introduce sustainability filters, green badges, and purpose-led content series. Partner with Australian environmental initiatives to compete with Adidas's ESG brand equity.
